Egypt Shops Privacy Policy

Information from merchants
  • We collect your name, address, email address, and mobile phone number.
  • We need this information to provide you with our services. At the forefront: Verifying your identity, contacting you and issuing invoices to you.
  • We collect data from the Egypt Shops web pages hosted and visited by you. We also collect data about how and when you access your account, including information about the device, the browser you are using, the network connection, and the IP address.
  • We need this information to provide and improve our services.
  • Upon completion of the service registration process, you will be able to activate the logistics and payment services in your account. If you do so, we will collect your address or addresses, your business type, business identification number, date of birth (if you are an individual business owner) and bank account information and official personal identification/identification information.
  • We need this information to establish logistics and payment services for you, including fraud and risk monitoring and compliance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
  • We collect personal information about your customers that you share with us or that customers provide during shopping or during the payment process.
  • We will also use personal information in other cases if we give your explicit permission.
  • Address book: You can provide us with the phone numbers of your store users and other contacts in your mobile phone address book. You confirm that you are authorized to provide us with these numbers to allow us to provide our services.
  • Camera and Photo Library: Provide us with access to your camera and photo library. You confirm that you are authorized to provide us with these images to allow us to provide our services.
  • Site: You provide our access to your site using the location service on your phone and confirm that you are authorized to provide us with these sites to allow us to provide our services.
  • Use and registration of information: We collect information related to services, diagnosis, and performance, and this includes information about your activity such as how to use our services, how you interact with others using our services and the like, log files, diagnostics, malfunctions, websites, performance logs, and reports.
  • Device and contact information: We collect device-specific information when installing, accessing or using our services. This includes information such as device type, operating system information, browser information, IP address, and mobile network information including phone number and device identifiers. We collect device location information if you use our location features.
Information from Egypt Shops visitors and support users
  • We collect information from the Egypt Shops website visitors about the device, the browser that uses it, the network connection, and its IP address. We may also receive personal information when sending requests, support tickets or other requests to Egypt Shops via any of our websites.
  • We collect phone numbers and record calls from support phone users.
  • We collect name, email address, device and browser information, network connection, IP address and chat text from chat support users.
  • We collect the name, email address, and store URL from store and app users.
Information from cookies and similar tracking technology
  • We use cookies to recognize your device to provide you with a personalized experience.
  • We may use cookies to display personalized ads from Google and other third-party vendors.
  • We may also use web beacons, tracking technology, and other automated tracking methods on our websites to communicate with you and our products and services to measure performance and engagement.
  • We work and provide our services including providing customer support, improving, repairing and customizing our services. We understand how people use our services to analyze and use our information to evaluate and improve our services, research and development, test services and new features, and conduct troubleshooting activities. We also use your information to respond to you when you contact us. We also use cookies to operate, improve, understand and customize our services.
What do we do with your personal information when you end your relationship with us?
  • We will continue to store archived copies of your personal information for legitimate business purposes and in compliance with laws and regulations.
  • We will continue to store anonymous information such as website visits without identifiers to improve our services.
How can we keep your personal information safe?
  • We follow industry standards in Information Security Management to protect sensitive information such as financial information, employee details, and any other personal information entrusted to us. Our information security systems apply to people, processes and IT systems on a risk management basis.
  • A qualified third-party to manage the payment gateway manages and checks our payment gateways.
  • There is no online transmission method or a 100% secure online storage method. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.
